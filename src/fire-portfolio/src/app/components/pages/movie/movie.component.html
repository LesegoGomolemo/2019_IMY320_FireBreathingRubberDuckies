Synopsis

The story follows a group of young students, Jared, Kate and Jess, who have volunteered to cleanup a local theater and it all begins on one fateful afternoon. One of the students discovers a little puppet on strings while cleaning up. and come back the following day, only notice that the puppet's strings have been cut off. It's shrugged off. During their lunch break, the students hear some commotion coming from backstage. They walk in to investigate. A ball bounces onto the stage from nowhere and the team is on edge. They finish packing and grab their bags to leave but not before they notice a stranger standing with their back towards them. The man's head turns 180 degrees, exposing a face resembling a puppet. Trying to escape the find doors locked and the man snatches away one of their peers, Jared. She girls escape and meet up with Mr de Villiers, their lecturer. They drag him away and explain.Mr de Villiers settles their fears and they go in for Jared; meeting closed stage curtains, they slowly open them up, and in front of their eyes sits Jared, dead, with a rope around his neck and the puppet doll on his thighs.
