Synopsis

Volunteer work can be long and boring for young university students. This is the predicament 3 friends find themselves in while cleaning up the local theater’s backstage, that is until they find a small, old, creepy puppet. The students soon find out that the puppet it much more than just a drama prop and must try to survive.
